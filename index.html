About Us

Cooking since 1990

Established in 1954, Burger King has been a pioneer in the fast-food industry, revolutionizing the way people enjoy their favorite meals. At Burger King, we're more than just a fast-food restaurant – we're a brand that's committed to serving up flame-grilled perfection with every bite. Our iconic Whopper® sandwich, made with 100% beef, fresh ingredients, and our signature flame-grilling technique, has been delighting taste buds for decades. But we're not just about burgers – our menu offers a wide range of options to satisfy every craving, from crispy chicken sandwiches to savory sides and refreshing beverages.
